SELF DRIVING CAR

My homemade self driving car, June, 2016

Self-driving cars have always intrigued me. Over the summer I decided it would be cool to look into how they work. My research quickly led me to want to explore the algorithms that power self-driving cars. I learned a lot about computer vision and how it can be applied to roads, which inspired me to try designing my own system. Sitting across my desk was a computer I had previously built inside a tupperware container. Looking at it made me think it was the perfect size to fit in a vehicle and control it.
